◆ HSI48_VALUE

#define HSI48_VALUE    ( 48000000UL )

Internal High Speed oscillator (HSI48) value for USB FS and RNG.

This internal oscillator is mainly dedicated to provide a high precision clock to the USB peripheral by means of a special Clock Recovery System (CRS) circuitry. When the CRS is not used, the HSI48 RC oscillator runs on it default frequency which is subject to manufacturing process variations. Value of the Internal High Speed oscillator for USB FS/RNG in Hz. \ The real value my vary depending on manufacturing process \ variations.
